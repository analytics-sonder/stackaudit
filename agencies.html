The agency opportunity

How a stack audit fits into agency services

Agencies that offer martech audits report it as one of their highest-value, lowest-effort deliverables. Here's why.

Positions you as strategic

A detailed, scored audit with specific savings figures changes the client conversation from "execute our campaigns" to "advise on our entire marketing operation." That's a premium retainer, not a project fee.

Creates natural consulting leads

Once a client sees they're wasting $800/month across their stack, they want someone to fix it. The audit finding directly drives implementation work — CRM optimisation, automation setup, tool consolidation.

Repeatable — monthly or quarterly

A stack audit isn't a one-time deliverable. Tools change. Teams grow. Plans change. Running a quarterly review for retained clients adds ongoing value with very little incremental effort.

Differentiates from generic agencies

Most agencies don't look at the client's tool spend at all. Walking into a pitch with a structured audit framework — even before you're hired — immediately separates you from competitors.
