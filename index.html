FAQ

Common questions

What is a Stack Efficiency Score?
The Stack Efficiency Score (0-100) is a deterministic measure of how optimally your marketing stack is configured. It evaluates cost vs category benchmarks, seat utilization, feature adoption, tool overlap, and billing efficiency using fixed weights. The same inputs always produce the same score - no AI randomness. Top 10% of B2B teams score 85+. Industry average is 58/100.
How does StackAudit differ from asking an AI chatbot?
AI chatbots give generic advice: "You might have overlap." StackAudit gives exact calculations: "HubSpot + Mailchimp have 70% feature overlap - you are paying Rs 8,000/month for duplicate email automation." The difference is structured financial clarity backed by real benchmark data, not suggestions generated from training data.
How much do B2B companies waste on marketing tools?
Research consistently shows B2B marketing teams waste 30-50% of their MarTech budget. The most common causes are unused seat licenses (you bought 10, 6 people use it), overlapping tools in the same category, overbuilt plan tiers, monthly billing where annual would save 20%, and auto-renewing contracts nobody reviews before they fire.
Is StackAudit free?
Yes. The Free plan supports up to 3 tools with Stack Efficiency Scoring and industry benchmark comparison - no sign-up required. The Full Report is a one-time $10 payment that unlocks unlimited tools, complete financial breakdown in Rs/$, tool overlap map with percentages, alternative recommendations, renewal alerts, and PDF export to share with leadership.
Does StackAudit need access to my tools or accounts?
No. StackAudit is entirely manual-input based. You enter the details - tool name, cost, seats, usage - and the scoring engine does the rest. No integrations, no API keys, no read access to any of your tools. Most audits complete in under 10 minutes.
