What StackAudit does

Every dimension of marketing tool waste — scored and explained.

Not a generic cost calculator. Every recommendation comes with a specific reason and an estimated monthly saving.

Smart renewal alerts

Tell us when you subscribed. We infer the renewal date from your billing cycle — no date picker required. Monthly tools get a 25-day alert. Annual tools get 60 days advance notice.

Plan tier optimization

Are you on Enterprise but using Starter-level features? We flag every tool where you're overbuying, identify the right plan tier, and estimate the exact monthly downgrade saving.

Seat utilization tracking

Track seats purchased vs active users per tool. When utilization drops below 65%, StackAudit recommends exact seat reductions with the monthly saving per tool.

Add-on waste audit

Log every paid add-on per tool. We cross-reference what's active vs what's actually used. Premium support, advanced reporting, SSO — if it's unused, you'll see it flagged instantly.

Category overlap detection

StackAudit maps every tool to a category and surfaces when multiple tools serve identical functions. Three prospecting databases? We'll show you which to benchmark and which to cut.

AI insights — Scale plan

Claude-powered stack analysis. Cross-tool patterns, consolidation scenarios, ROI estimates, and a conversational follow-up interface for deeper investigation into any tool or category.

How it works

Audit your full stack in under 10 minutes.

No integrations required. No IT involvement. Add tools manually with exactly the details that matter for accurate scoring.

1

Add your tools

Name, category, cost, plan tier, seats purchased, active users, billing cycle, add-ons. About 60 seconds per tool. No account connection needed.

2

We score every tool

Instant 9-dimension optimization score covering cost weight, usage frequency, seat utilization, plan fit, feature adoption, category overlap, data usage, billing cycle, and business criticality.

3

Get specific actions

Not "reduce costs." Actual specifics: "Reduce ZoomInfo to 2 seats at renewal — saves $280/mo." Every tool. Every recommendation. Every saving explained.

4

Act before renewal

Renewal alerts fire automatically before each tool renews. Optimize seats, downgrade plan, or switch to annual billing — before the contract locks for another year.

The scoring model

9 dimensions. One honest score.

Every tool gets an optimization score from 0–100. The higher the score, the more waste potential. The lower, the more justified the spend. We always explain why — never just a number.
